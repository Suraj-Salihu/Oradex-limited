About ORADEX LIMITED

About Us

Oradex Limited is a multidisciplinary company committed to delivering innovative, sustainable, and people-centered solutions across multiple sectors. Founded with the vision of creating impact-driven enterprises, Oradex Limited operates with the core values of trust, diversity, and innovation while seeking to bridge gaps between people, businesses, and opportunities.

Our Mission

To provide innovative and sustainable solutions that empower individuals, businesses, and communities while upholding trust, diversity, and long-term value creation.

Our Vision

To be a trusted and diversified brand that delivers excellence across multiple industries and leaves a positive footprint in every community we serve.

Core Values

  • Trust – We build relationships on integrity and transparency.
  • Diversity – We embrace opportunities across industries and communities.
  • Innovation – We create solutions that address today's needs and anticipate tomorrow's.
  • Sustainability – We prioritize long-term growth and community impact.

Motto: "Trust with Diversity"

Onalaja Rasheed A.

CEO & Founder

Holds a Bachelor's degree in Plant Physiology and Crop Production from the Federal University of Agriculture, Abeokuta, and a mini-MBA from Tekedia Institute.

Onyinye Okugo

Company Advisor

Onyinye Okugo is a Business Consultant and Product Strategist with a track record of mentoring emerging entrepreneurs, supporting startups, and leading product development.
She offers strategic guidance, product insights, marketing strategy, leadership and organizational development, and problem-solving support for startups.

An alumna of the YALI Regional Leadership Center West Africa and a member of several impact-driven organizations, Onyinye is passionate about business growth, innovation, technology, and humanitarianism.
